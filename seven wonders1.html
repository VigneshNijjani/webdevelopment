1.GREAT WALL OF CHINA

The Great Wall of China is the longest man-made structure in the world. It was built to protect China from invasions. The wall stretches across mountains, deserts, and valleys. Many dynasties worked on its construction over centuries. It is made of stone, brick, and earth. Soldiers once guarded it day and night. Today, it stands as a symbol of China’s strength and history.

2.PETRA

Petra is an ancient city located in Jordan. It is famous for its buildings carved into red rock cliffs. Petra was once an important trading center. The city remained hidden for many years. The Treasury is the most popular monument there. It shows advanced architecture of ancient times. Petra is also known as the Rose City.

3.CHRIST THE REDEEMERS

Christ the Redeemer is a huge statue in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It is located on Mount Corcovado. The statue represents Jesus Christ. Its outstretched arms symbolize peace and love. It is made of reinforced concrete and soapstone. The statue attracts millions of visitors every year. It is a major symbol of Brazil and Christianity.

4.MACHU PICCHU

Machu Picchu is an ancient Incan city in Peru. It is located high in the Andes Mountains. The city is famous for its stone buildings and terraces. It was unknown to the outside world for centuries. Machu Picchu shows the intelligence of Incan engineering. It is surrounded by natural beauty. Today, it is a popular tourist destination.

5.CHICHEN ITZA

Chichen Itza is an ancient Mayan city in Mexico. It is famous for the pyramid called El Castillo. The structure is connected to Mayan astronomy. During equinoxes, a snake-shaped shadow appears on the pyramid. Chichen Itza was a major religious center. The Mayans had advanced scientific knowledge. It is one of Mexico’s most visited historical sites.

6.COLOSSEUM

The Colosseum is a large amphitheater in Rome, Italy. It was built during the Roman Empire. Gladiator fights and public events were held there. Thousands of people gathered to watch the games. The Colosseum is made of stone and concrete. It shows excellent Roman engineering. Today, it is a symbol of ancient Rome.

7.TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, India. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan. The monument was built in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is made of white marble. The Taj Mahal looks different at different times of the day. It is famous for its perfect symmetry and design. The Taj Mahal is a symbol of love.
